How It Works

Three steps, two clean paths for buyers and creators.

SIWE (EIP-4361) is used for wallet sign-in. x402 is used for payment.

1. Buy a Soul

Browse public OpenClaw listings and select the SOUL.md profile that matches your agent identity.

2. Pay and Reuse

Authorize payment with x402 on Base once, then re-download later with wallet proof and no repay.

3. Publish Instantly

Connect wallet, complete four fields, and publish immediately with a shareable link.
